Hey there! I'm Alec Eckert, this website of mine is here to demonstrate my GIS Maps.

This website holds a series of different Maps, which serve as a repertoire of my skills with Geospacial technologies. I produced these using Geographical Information Systems, each map is created to better understand the UN's Sustainable Development Goal 15.1. Life On Land.
